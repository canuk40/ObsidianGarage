Privacy Policy — Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Overview

ObsidianGarage ("the App", "we", "our") is a vehicle maintenance tracker for Android, developed and published by [YOUR NAME / COMPANY NAME]. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights with respect to that information.

Short version: your vehicle data stays on your device. We do not sell your data. We do not serve ads. Optional cloud features require a Google account and are only active when you enable them.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Data you create in the App

Vehicle records, service logs, reminders, odometer readings, cost data, notes, and receipt photos you add are stored locally on your device using an encrypted SQLite database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable Cloud Sync or Cloud Backup (see Section 4).

2b. Anonymous usage analytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) to collect anonymous, aggregate usage statistics — for example, which features are used most often, how often a service log is added, or whether a backup is triggered. This data cannot be used to identify you individually. No vehicle records, cost data, or personally identifiable information (PII) is ever sent to Firebase Analytics.

You can opt out of analytics collection at any time through the Settings → Notifications & Privacy section of the App, or via your device's "Opt out of Ads Personalisation" system setting.

2c. Purchase information

In-app purchases (Pro, Lifetime, Annual subscription) are processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We receive only a confirmation that a purchase has been made and the associated product ID. We do not collect or store payment card numbers, billing addresses, or any other payment details.

2d. Account information (optional, Premium only) Pro / Lifetime

If you choose to use Cloud Sync or Cloud Backup, you sign in with your Google account via Firebase Authentication. We store your Google account's User ID (UID) and email address in Firebase to associate your data with your account. We do not access your Google contacts, Calendar, Drive files, or any other Google account data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Vehicle & maintenance data — to power the core features of the App (logs, reminders, statistics, PDF/CSV export).
  • Analytics — to understand how the App is used and improve it over time.
  • Purchase status — to unlock Pro or Lifetime features you have purchased.
  • Google account (optional) — to authenticate you and route your sync and backup data to your private cloud storage bucket.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose not listed above.

4. Cloud Sync & Cloud Backup Lifetime / Annual

Cloud Sync and Cloud Backup are optional Premium features. When enabled:

  • Your vehicle records, service logs, and reminders are stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage (Google LLC, USA) under a path unique to your Google UID. No other user can access your data.
  • Backup files are encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest by Firebase's default encryption.
  • You can delete all cloud data at any time from Settings → Account → Delete cloud data, or by deleting your account.
  • If you sign out or cancel your subscription, we stop syncing. Your local data on-device is not affected.

Firebase infrastructure is operated by Google LLC. For Google's own privacy practices see policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data Retention

  • Local data — retained until you uninstall the App or clear app data.
  • Firebase Analytics — retained for 14 months per Google's default, after which it is automatically deleted.
  • Cloud Sync / Backup data — retained until you delete it from within the App or delete your Firebase account. We will delete your cloud data within 30 days of an account deletion request.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes. We use only the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Billing — purchase processing (Terms)
  • Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage analytics (Privacy)
  • Firebase Authentication — optional sign-in (Premium only)
  • Firebase Firestore & Storage — optional cloud sync/backup (Premium only)

All of the above are operated by Google LLC. We have no other third-party data processors.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have rights including:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data (see Section 5).
  • Portability — export your vehicle data as CSV or PDF from within the App.
  • Opt-out of analytics — disable Firebase Analytics in the App settings or via your device's system settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in Section 10.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data: local storage uses Android's application sandbox, cloud data is transmitted over TLS, and Firebase storage is encrypted at rest. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
